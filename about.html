about

Hi there! As you have probably gathered, my name is Hannah Fass. I'm a graphic designer with a passion for branding and identity, and a developing interest in web design. For the past 9 years I've slowly come to learn about the many facets of design; some self-taught and some through an academic setting. Presently, I am enrolled in my last semester studying Graphic Design at Madison College and will receive an Associates Degree in Applied Arts in May of 2012.

I am currently living in Madison, WI with my partner Neil and cat Vinnie. In my free time I enjoy watching Netflix, surfing the web, and partaking in the many fun activities the Madison area has to offer (e.g. Sun Prairie's Corn Fest).
