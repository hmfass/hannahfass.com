about

Hi there! As you have probably gathered, my name is Hannah Fass. I'm a graphic designer with a passion for typography, logo design, and identidy design. For the past 9 years I've slowly come to learn about the many facets of design; some self-taught and some through an academic setting. In May of 2012, I recieved an Associates Degree in Applied Arts from Madison College.Presently, I am a Graphic Designer at Firepower Design.

I am currently living in Madison, WI with my partner Neil. In my free time I enjoy watching Netflix, surfing the web, and partaking in the many fun activities the Madison area has to offer (e.g. Sun Prairie's Corn Fest). changes
